Defining Decade

  • 1) INTRODUCTION - 80% of life’s most significant events takes place by age 35, therefore 20’s are the time when you can define your life, we can create our life we hope for, experience new things, Enjoy things, Create stories that will live throughout life, and create relationship. The 20’s are real time and ought to be lives that way.
  • 2) CRITICAL PERIOD – A time when we are primed to growth and change, and simple exposure can lead to dramatic transformation. It’s easiest to start the lives in 20’s that we want, these are the inflection point, the experience we have disproportionately influence the adult life we will lead.
  • 3) WEAK TIES – The urban tribe help us survive, the weak ties help us thrive i.e. getting new information, access to something fresh knowledge. According to Ben Franklin effect, on favour begets more favours and overtime small favours beget larger one.
  • 4) THE CUSTOMISED LIFE – Those who can tell a good story about who they are and what they want, leads over those who can’t, you cannot pull out some great career out of a hat in 30’s you have to start in your 20’s, These are the times we have more self-defining memories and relationship, The story we create become facets of our identity, you have to customised your life accordingly.
  • 5) The Brain and the Body – (Forward Thinking) – our frontal lobe is responsible for forward thinking, it is about how you think and act in an uncertain situation, these are the time when we develop our forward thinking, 20’s are very sensitive to whatever is within earshot,. • 20’s job teach us about regulating our emotions & Negotiations,. • 20’s work and school are our best chance to acquire the technical sophistication skills for career • 20’s relationship preparing us for marriage & other partnership. • 20’s plan helps us to think across the years & decade ahead.
  • 6) Great Opportunity – 20’s is time of great risk and opportunity, Never again will be so quick to learn new things, never again will it be so easy to become the people we hope to be, the risk is we may not act now, 20’s having good jobs and real relationship are learning the language of adulthood, 20’s are time to get busy, its forward thinking for an uncertain age.
  • 7) CALM YOURSELF –you have to get some roots & stand in the wind in 20’s as these are the times when we have the most remarkable, bizarre & highly emotional memories, 20’s is full of surprises and new memories. Research shows that people who have control over their emotions report greater life satisfaction, optimism, purpose & better relationship.
  • 8) GETTING ALONG AND GETTING AHEAD –Relatively speaking people don’t change after 30’s, our personality change more during the 20’s year than any time before and after, these are the best time for change, from socially anxious to socially confident, also having GOALS can make us happier.
  • 9) DO THE MATH – Be ruled by time, the wisest counsellor of all, There Is a big difference in having life in 30’s and starting life in 30’s. Most 20’s cannot write the last sentence of their lives, but when pressed, they usually can identify things they want in their 30’s, 40’s or 60’s, or things they don’t want, and then worked backward.
  • 10) WILL THINGS WORK OUT FOR ME – The future isn’t written in stars, these are no guarantee so calm your adulthood, be intentional. Get to work pick your family, do the math, make your own certainty and don’t be defined by what you don’t know, you are deciding your life RIGHT NOW.
